OUR RESEARCH JOURNEY

To accomplish our task, our team implemented AI called a convolutional neural network. Convolution is very good at outlining
or getting the edges of the object in an image. Our AI essentially uses convolution to reduce the information to the most important parts and classifies objects in
the image into 7 categories: plastic, glass, paper, organic, metal, e-waste, and just trash.

Winter

January 2023 - February 2023

We developed a beginner model in Tensorflow that has 78% accuracy. It had 1 sequential layer with 3 inner layers.

Summer I

June 2023 - July 2023

We switched to Pytorch since Tensorflow did not allow us to use graphics (GPU). With new members in our team, we began to implement keyword API, build a webpage, and expand on object detection. Our model has progressed to have 80% accuracy.

Summer II

July 2023 - August 2023

We constructed a database and established algorithms for API. This filters the algorithm in order to find the best category match. We also added the finishing touches to our AI model and prepared our website for deployment. Our model now has 95% accuracy.
